Bhartiya Computer Saksharta Abhiyan (BCSA) is a leading Global Talent Development Company, building skilled manpower pool for global industry requirements. The company which was set up in 2012, to help the nascent IT industry overcome its human resource challenges, has today grown to be amongst world’s leading talent development companies offering learning solutions to Individuals, Enterprises and Institutions across 35 countries.
